# Security Policy

## Supported Versions
Security fixes are provided on `main` until the first stable release. After GA, only the latest minor will receive fixes unless otherwise announced.

## Reporting a Vulnerability
- Email: security@sitaramtech.in
- PGP: Optional, request our key via email
- Response SLA: We acknowledge within 3 business days and provide a timeline within 7 business days.

Please include:
- Affected components, version/commit
- Reproduction steps or PoC
- Impact assessment
- Your contact details for follow-up

Do not publicly disclose vulnerabilities before a coordinated fix and release. We follow responsible disclosure.

## Bounty
At present, no formal bounty program. Good-faith reports may be recognized in `NOTICE`.

## Legal Safe Harbor
We will not pursue legal action for good-faith, non-destructive testing limited to this project, consistent with Indian law, provided you: avoid privacy violations, data exfiltration, and service disruption; and promptly report findings.
